About

SoulScape is a haloween-themed game that I made for the Dresscode Haloween Coding Competition 2023. I made it in the Unity Game Engine and coded it in C#. You have to complete a variety of levels that gradually progress in difficulty by collecting all of the pumpkins on each level.

Controls

Move Left
Move Right
Jump
Restart Level
